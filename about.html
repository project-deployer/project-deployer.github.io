About Grade Calculator

Simplifying Academic Success for Future Engineers

Grade Calculator is a free, student-led online educational portal designed to help university students navigate the complexities of 10-point grading systems, specifically optimized for Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) curriculums.

Who We Are

A community-driven platform created by students to help peers manage academic records effectively.

Fast & Precise

Calculations are performed instantly in your browser. No registration or data collection required.

Device Ready

Fully responsive design that works perfectly on mobile phones, tablets, and desktop computers.

Our Mission

To empower students with transparent academic tracking tools. We aim to reduce the stress of manual calculations, allowing students to focus more on learning and less on math errors.

Who Can Benefit?

  • Engineering Students: Especially those in AIML, CSE, and ECE branches.
  • RGUKT Scholars: Specifically tailored to the unique credit systems of RGUKT campuses.
  • Educators: Quick tool for verifying grade distributions.

Any reference to educational institutions (like RGUKT AP) is for informational and comparative purposes only. For official results, always refer to your university's student portal.
