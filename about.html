Who We Are

Grade Calculator is an independent educational website created to help students quickly calculate grades, percentages, GPA, and CGPA. This tool is especially useful for engineering and technology students, including those studying Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML).

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify academic calculations and reduce errors that occur during manual grade computation. We aim to provide a free, simple, and reliable platform that students can use anytime without registration.

Who Can Use This Website

  • Engineering students
  • AI & ML students
  • College students and educators

Why Choose Our Grade Calculator

This website is designed with simplicity and speed in mind. It works smoothly on mobile phones, tablets, and laptops. We continuously improve the calculator based on student feedback and academic needs.
