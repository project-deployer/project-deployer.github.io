About Grade Calculator

Grade Calculator is a free online educational tool designed to help students calculate grades, percentages, GPA, and CGPA quickly and accurately.

Who We Are

This website is created and maintained by students with the aim of simplifying academic calculations. It is especially useful for engineering and technology students, including those studying Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML).

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce errors caused by manual grade calculations and to provide a simple, fast, and reliable platform that students can use without registration or payment.

Who Can Use This Website

  • School students
  • Engineering students
  • AI & ML students
  • College students and educators

Why Choose Our Tool

Grade Calculator works smoothly on mobile phones, tablets, and laptops. It is lightweight, easy to use, and continuously improved based on student needs.
