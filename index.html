Grade Calculator for RGUKT AP Students (AI & ML)

Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies (RGUKT), Andhra Pradesh (AP) offers engineering programs in emerging fields such as Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML). Students from RGUKT AP often need quick and accurate ways to calculate grades, percentages, and academic performance for self-evaluation.

Why Grade Calculation Is Important for AI & ML Students

AI and ML students study subjects like Python Programming, Data Structures, Statistics, Linear Algebra, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning. Tracking academic performance across these subjects helps students understand their strengths and areas that need improvement.

This online grade calculator helps students estimate grades based on marks obtained in examinations. It reduces manual calculation errors and saves time.

How Students Can Use This Grade Calculator

Who Can Use This Tool?

This tool is designed for engineering students, including those studying at RGUKT AP, who want a simple and fast way to calculate grades. It works on mobile phones and laptops without requiring login or registration.
