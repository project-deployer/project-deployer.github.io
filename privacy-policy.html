Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2, 2026.

Independent Educational Platform

This website is an independent educational tool created and maintained by students. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to any university or government institution. Our goal is strictly to provide a free utility for academic calculation.

No Personal Data Collection

We believe in complete data sovereignty.

  • Calculation Data: All grades and credits you enter remain in your browser's temporary memory. We never see or store your academic marks.
  • Personal Info: We do not ask for names, roll numbers, or contact details.

Log Files

Like most websites, we follow a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, and referring/exit pages. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Advertising & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to show advertisements to keep this tool free for everyone. Google uses DART cookies to serve ads based on your visit to this site.

You can manage your preferences or opt-out of these cookies by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy.

CCPA & GDPR Rights

We want to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access: You can request copies of any data we hold (which is zero).
  • The right to erasure: Since we don't store your data, your data is "erased" the moment you close your browser tab.

Children's Information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity. This tool does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13.
